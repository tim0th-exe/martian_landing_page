Climate Heroes Project

Climate Heroes showcases 12 digital characters, each embodying endangered species profoundly affected by the climate emergency. These characters find themselves in dynamic and wild amalgamations of their natural ecosystems, vividly illustrating the potential consequences if climate targets are not met.

Our project goes beyond the surface, delving into the physiological changes that these endangered species face due to the predicted 1.5Â°C+ increase in global temperatures. Climate Heroes isn't just art; it's an immersive and accessible experience designed to convey empirical information about the climate emergency. Our goal is to inspire awareness, empathy, and action, urging collective efforts to mitigate the effects of the climate crisis.

Project Inspiration

The Climate Heroes Project finds its roots in the urgent call to action outlined by the WWF in their report, "Feeling the Heat: The Fate of Nature Beyond 1.5 Degrees of Global Warming." This comprehensive report highlights the critical consequences of surpassing the 1.5-degree threshold and the profound impact on the world's ecosystems and endangered species.

Responding to the sobering insights provided by the WWF report, our project serves as a bridge between complex data and a broader audience. By translating intricate research into immersive and emotionally resonant art, we aim to engage diverse communities, fostering awareness, empathy, and a collective commitment to address the challenges posed by the climate emergency.
