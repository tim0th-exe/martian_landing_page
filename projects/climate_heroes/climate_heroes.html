Climate Heroes Project

Climate Heroes showcases 12 digital characters representing endangered species most affected by the climate emergency. These characters, situated in wild and turbulent amalgamations of their respective ecosystems, highlight the environmental impact on endangered species if climate targets are not met.

The project sheds light on the physiological changes threatened by the predicted 1.5Â°C+ increase in global temperatures. Climate Heroes serves as an immersive and accessible experience to engage with empirical information regarding the climate emergency, aiming to encourage awareness, empathy, and action towards mitigating the effects of the climate emergency.
